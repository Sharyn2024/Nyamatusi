Our story

Nyamatusi Camp is our flagship in terms of its Eco-conscious style. Our Mana Pools safari lodge is set in one of the wildest and most remote parts of Africa, on the banks of the Zambezi, one of the world’s oldest rivers. Embracing comfort, this property embodies sustainable design. Fully solar-powered and built from all-natural materials it encompasses its wilderness location in the most elegant way. Here, your Mana Pools safari plays out against a sweeping riverine landscape, where epic encounters of the giants that roam this land await.

Our activities

Game drives

Game drives are what gets us up in the morning in the African bush. They are a great avenue for exploring the land in a safe and exciting way

Walking safari

Imagine the earth crunching beneath your feet, the distant roar of a lion echoing through the savanna, and the thrill of spotting wildlife up close—without the barrier of a vehicle.y

Bird watching

Whether you’re a seasoned birder with a checklist of rare species or a beginner marveling at the beauty in your own backyard, every fluttering wing and melodic call tells a story.

Canoeing

Canoeing isn’t just a sport—it’s an escape into the heart of nature.So grab your paddle, steady your canoe, and let the current guide you to unforgettable experiences.

Fishing

Whether you’re a seasoned angler chasing the biggest catch or a beginner seeking the peace of a quiet lake, the world of fishing offers endless excitement and discovery.

Recent wildlife sightings

Wildlife sightings include elephant, kudu, sable antelope, zebra, wildebeest, the rare and endangered roan antelope, buffalo, lion, leopard, hyena, and many other wildlife species.
